Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Perfectly chewy and crispy cookies with a rich chocolate flavor

Chef Sarah Johnson
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 12 min
Total: 27 min
Serves: 24 cookies

Ingredients

2¼ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup butter, softened
¾ cup granulated sugar
¾ cup packed brown sugar
2 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups chocolate chips

Method

1
Preheat and Prepare
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2
Mix Dry Ingredients
In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
3
Cream Butter and Sugars
In a large bowl, beat softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
4
Add Eggs and Vanilla
Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.
5
Combine Ingredients
Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
6
Bake Cookies
Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden brown.

Additional Information

Nutrition Facts
Calories: 120 per cookie
Fat: 6g
Carbs: 15g
Protein: 2g
Difficulty
★★★★★
Easy
Tips
Use room temperature eggs
Don't overbake for chewy texture
Store in airtight container
